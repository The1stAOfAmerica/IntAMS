About Us

What is IntAMS?

IntAMS, or the International Advance Math Society, is a wonderful place to be for all things MATH! We help with your current school topics, relieving the stress or rigor of your math class, or, we can help you learn ahead so your thirst for knowledge can be quenched :). We have mountains of resources available for club members and wonderful teachers who thoroughly know the material and, of course, are willing to help. Feel free to check us out!
